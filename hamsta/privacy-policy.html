Privacy Policy for Dzeta Kombat

Effective Date: 25.11.2024

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect personal information from users. However, it uses local storage on your device to save your game progress, such as:

  • Number of clicks (score).

This data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Information

The data stored on your device is used solely for:

  • Displaying your current game progress.

We do not use this data for analytics, advertising, or any other purposes.

3. Third-Party Access

The App does not share your information with third parties. No personal or gameplay data is transmitted over the internet.

4. Your Data Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can clear your game progress by manually clearing your browser's local storage.
  • This will reset your score to zero.

5. Security

The App does not store or process any sensitive data. All information is kept locally on your device and cannot be accessed remotely.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
